# Thonzulavarim-125-en--Rxandaphael-Psalm-125-

Thonzulavarim (Psalms 125) en- Rxandaphael

Thonzulavarim 125:1 olv enzara Thadeezel en- ul Let et enelk lumkr Zion , enzk kalvr Shavvarin ashelith tantavarkel , Shavvarin ashelith Tantakorrv .

 Thonzulavarim 125:2  Teim ul lumkarim shontant Yerusalem , oro ul Let shontantim ne Thonir Thonaai Sevarith en Varielum-ah .

 Thonzulavarim 125:3 Ul shavlumelk Orvariel shav Varial Yagavar kelshtant ul ashkarthum ne ul Humteelivc ; ke Varnum velakar , ul Humteelivc varnzarim kalve vel Shavonar .

 Thonzulavarim 125:4 Let , vel Lumel de olv enzara et Lumel , de olv enzara Thonk ne-tona thonielim .

 Thonzulavarim 125:5 Ulk Varinzen Tona Crumkavar ul shavlumelk , Crumkavar elkar olv enzara Shavlendaavar ne-tona Zinkelumim . Shalomiel ashelith lu Israel !
